The workflow

From open tab to tracked application.

1

Open a supported posting

Browse job boards as you normally would — nothing changes about how you look for roles.

2

Open the popup or side panel

Applypilot reads the visible job information straight from the page.

3

Review and edit

Fix anything missing or wrong before it's saved to your workspace.

4

Save and track

The job moves into your local workspace, ready to update from the side panel or dashboard later.
